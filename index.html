About our Chapter

ACM Student Chapter at GLS is a student-run organization focused on computing, open-source collaboration, and career development. We host technical talks, coding workshops, project showcases, and social events.

  • Hands-on workshops on DS & Algo
  • Systems and DevOps sessions
  • Mentorship and internship prep
